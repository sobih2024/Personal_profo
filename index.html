About Me

Software Developer & Designer

Hello! I'm Ahmad Sobih, a dedicated and passionate IT professional based in Mishawaka, IN. I hold a Bachelor of Science in Informatics with a Cognate in New Media/Art from Indiana University South Bend, where I also earned certificates in Computer Programming and Computer Applications.

My journey in the tech world has equipped me with a robust set of skills, including proficiency in various programming languages such as Java, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, SQL, Dart, PHP, and C++. I'm also adept in operating systems like Windows, Mac OS, and Linux, and have hands-on experience with software tools such as NetBeans, Visual Studio Code, Flutter, and Processing 4. Additionally, I'm well-versed in the Microsoft Office Suite, including Excel, Word, Access, and PowerPoint. As a bilingual professional, I bring the added advantage of fluency in both Arabic and English.

Currently, I am working as a Web Technician with the IT Services Help Desk at Indiana University South Bend. In this role, I collaborate with the University Information Technology Services and the Office of Communications and Marketing to enhance and maintain the IU South Bend website. My responsibilities include monitoring ticket requests to ensure timely updates, migrating webpage content using HTML and CSS, and reviewing pages for adherence to IU's style guide and branding. I also ensure the website's content is up-to-date and free of duplications, broken links, and navigation issues.
